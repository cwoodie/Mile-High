The Secret Weapon: Slick vs. Stick

Like choosing the perfect club, mastering cornhole requires knowing which side of the bag to use for the shot.

The Stick Side (The Gripper)

Purpose

Used for aggressive, high-arc throws where you need the bag to land and stop immediately around the hole.

Best For

Blockers, cut shots, and bags that need to stop quickly to avoid going off the back.

The Slick Side (The Slider)

Purpose

Used for low, fast, driving throws where you need the bag to slide up the board and push other bags into the hole.

Best For

Pushing bags, airmails, and clearing opponents' bags off the board.
